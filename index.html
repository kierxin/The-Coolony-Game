Game Instructions

Welcome to The Coolony Game! In this game, you're in charge of a colony of ants. Your colony already has some basic necessities set up, including a place for your ants to SLEEP, a place for them to EAT, and a NURSERY so your colony's population can continue to grow.

Everything in the colony doesn't just happen automatically, though. In the ANTS LIST, you can delegate tasks for each of your ants to carry out. These include harvesting FOOD, harvesting WOOD, harvesting CLAY, and a few other things you'll discover along the way.

Don't forget to make sure your ants don't starve or run out of energy. As time passes, each ant's ENERGY level depletes and its APPETITE increases. Have them EAT and SLEEP so that they can continue to do other tasks.

While your two worker ants have gotten by quite well on their own, you're going to need some more tunnels and resource deposits as your colony's population increases. You'll want to DIG. Luckily, one excavation site has already been established. Assign an ant to EXCAVATE at that site in order to reveal another tunnel or resource deposits.

When you decide to continue expanding your colony, enter BUILD MODE to choose the location of your next DIG site. It'll take some setting up, so you'll have to use some of the resources your ants have harvested to establish the new site. Once you have the new DIG site, you can assign an ant to EXCAVATE it.

That's all I'll say for now. If you're ready to get started, go down to the game window, choose a game speed, and open up the ANTS LIST to begin assigning tasks!
